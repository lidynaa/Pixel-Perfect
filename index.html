Frequently Asked Questions

Is PixelPerfect completely free to use?
Yes — 100% free, forever. No premium plans, no hidden fees, no credit card required, no watermarks. We are supported by non-intrusive advertising.
Does resizing reduce photo quality?
Downscaling always reduces some pixel information, but we use high-quality bicubic interpolation to maintain sharpness and color accuracy. Set the quality slider to 90–100 for best results. Enlarging beyond original size may introduce softness.
Are my photos uploaded to your servers?
Never. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas. Your images are never sent to any server. This also means it works offline after the page loads.
What is the maximum file size I can resize?
There is no strict server-side limit. Performance depends on your device's RAM. Files up to 50MB work smoothly on most modern computers. Very large RAW camera files (100MB+) may be slower on older devices.
Can I resize multiple photos at once?
Yes! Select multiple files in the file picker (hold Ctrl or Cmd) or drag and drop multiple images at once. All images will be resized with the same settings and downloaded individually.
